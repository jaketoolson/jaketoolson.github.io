Kabooodle.com Direct sales SaaS I created for my wife in 2016, to help her manage her direct sales inventory, and track and ship sales. Highlights: - Custom claim system - Payment gateway - USPS shipping integration and label creation - SMS Notifications - Like and Watch items, users - Referral system - Custom analytics - Facebook integration - Direct messaging system - Inventory grouping (build outfits with your existing inventory) Technology: LEMP, Laravel 5, Vue/x, MariaDB, AWS, Pusher, Lambda
